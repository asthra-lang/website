<section class="vibe-coding-section" id="vibe-coding-details">
  <div class="container">
    <h2 class="section-title">About "Vibe Coding"</h2>
    <div class="vibe-coding-content">
      <p>This project was created using <strong>"vibe coding"</strong>‚Äîa term coined by Andrej Karpathy describing an AI-dependent, improvisational approach to software development. In vibe coding, software is produced by describing problems in natural language to a large language model (LLM), often embracing the AI's suggestions without thorough review or understanding of the generated code.</p>
      
      <div class="vibe-coding-warning">
        <h3>Key Characteristics & Risks:</h3>
        <ul>
          <li><strong>Rapid Prototyping:</strong> Prioritizes speed over code quality</li>
          <li><strong>Minimal Review:</strong> Code may not be thoroughly tested or understood</li>
          <li><strong>Best Practices:</strong> May not follow established coding standards</li>
          <li><strong>Security Concerns:</strong> Potential vulnerabilities from unreviewed code</li>
          <li><strong>Maintainability:</strong> Codebase may be difficult to maintain long-term</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
      
      <div class="vibe-coding-appropriate">
        <h3>When Vibe Coding is Appropriate:</h3>
        <ul>
          <li>Low-stakes experimental projects</li>
          <li>Personal learning and exploration</li>
          <li>Rapid prototyping and proof-of-concepts</li>
          <li>Creative coding and artistic projects</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
